Dashboards

What kind of Reports

What We do

We support decision makers in service to all aspects of the university's mission through excellence in data management, reporting, and analysis. We empower the university community to make the highest and broadest use of all university data.

Dashboards

We collect dashboards from internal and build some of the dashboards with relevant ALU teams.

Reports

We report on NPS surveys done and conduct relevant analysis for different teams.

Analysis of survey responses

Both quantitative and qualitative analysis, immediately after surveys are closed.
